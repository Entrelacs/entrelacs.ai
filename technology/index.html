Meet the Technology

AI Powered Psychometrics for the modern world.

The Innovation


While significant research has focused on using large language models to generate psychological test items, we've taken the crucial next step: integrating LLMs directly into the assessment process itself. Current AI applications in mental health concentrate heavily on therapy chatbots and conversational interfaces—an increasingly saturated market. However, this focus overlooks a fundamental need: accurate, adaptive psychological assessments with clinical validity.


Our Approach


We address this critical gap by embedding advanced language models into the testing experience, enabling real-time, personalized question selection based on individual response patterns.

Dynamic Adaptation

Intelligent question selection that responds to individual response patterns

Enhanced Accuracy

More precise clinical insights through personalized assessment paths

Reduced Fatigue

Optimized question sequences minimize assessment burden

Clinical Validity

Maintained psychometric rigor with improved user engagement


Market Opportunity


The psychometric assessment space represents an underexplored opportunity in healthcare technology. While the market remains crowded with AI therapy solutions, psychological assessment—the foundation of all effective mental health interventions—has seen limited innovation.

Our technology creates the infrastructure that enhances all downstream mental health treatments by providing more accurate, engaging, and clinically meaningful assessments. This positions us uniquely in a market ready for transformation.

  • Corporate Wellness: Enterprise organizations seeking data-driven mental health solutions for employee screening, risk assessment, and wellness program effectiveness measurement. Our adaptive assessments provide actionable insights while maintaining employee privacy and engagement.
  • Third party integrations: As digital therapy becomes increasingly automated, robust analytics on intervention effectiveness become critical. Our assessments provide both end-users and platform providers with precise measurement tools to optimize treatment outcomes and validate therapeutic approaches.
  • Healthcare providers: Our strategic goal includes achieving medical device certification, positioning our technology as a validated clinical assessment tool for healthcare systems, psychiatric practices, and integrated care providers seeking evidence-based diagnostic support.

The Entrelacs Platform


The platform will be divided into two parts: an assessment app for clients to take the assessment and a web platform for mental health practitioners.

The App: From the App users are able to take weekly assessments and connect with their therapist, or sponsoring company. The mobile application provides a user-friendly interface for completing personalized psychological assessments, tracking progress over time, and maintaining seamless communication with healthcare providers.

The Platform: Here HR executives will be able to monitor the wellbeing of their workforce and therapists of their clients. This comprehensive dashboard offers real-time analytics, customizable reporting features, and insights into mental health trends, enabling data-driven decisions to support employee wellness programs and clinical practice management.

We are currently organizing beta tests with select organizations and healthcare providers to refine our platform and ensure it meets the highest standards of clinical effectiveness and user experience. If you are interested in participating or learning more about our solution, please contact us.
