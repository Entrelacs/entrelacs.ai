Our Technology

Four Core Components

Dynamic Psychometrics

Entrelacs uses dynamic psychometrics to capture how thoughts, emotions, and behaviors interact over time, revealing each person’s unique mental health fingerprint beyond what static questionnaires can show.

Learn more

Large Language Models(LLM) for Personalized Assessment

LLMs are AI systems trained on vast amounts of text, enabling them to understand context, generate natural responses, and conduct coherent conversations. Rather than just matching keywords, LLMs comprehend nuance, tone, and meaning.

Learn more

Behavioral Data Integration - Coming Soon

Behavioral data includes digital traces of daily life: communication frequency, activity levels, sleep patterns, location patterns, app usage. Services like Google Takeout allow users to export this data from platforms they already use.

Learn more

Multi-Dimensional Profiling - Coming Soon

Rather than screening for single conditions, the platform assesses multiple psychological dimensions simultaneously: social connection and loneliness, anxiety symptoms, depressive symptoms, stress and burnout indicators, and condition-specific symptomatology.
